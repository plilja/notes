Managing sessions
--------------------
tmux new -s {session-name}
tmux ls
tmux a -t {session-name}
tmux a  # attaches to last session


Windows/Tabs
--------------
# Create new
C-a c 
# Move next
C-a n
# Move prev
C-a p
# Kill
C-a &


Splits
---------------
Vertical
C-a v 

Horizontal
C-a s

Navigation
C-a hjkl
